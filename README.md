# Rewind

Time moves in one direction. You can't stop it. You can't slow it down. You can only watch it disappear.

This is a clock that runs backward. Because that's how time feels when you're running out of it.

---

## What this is

Not a productivity tool. Not a task manager. Not something that will help you "optimize" your day.

It's a mirror.

Rewind shows you:
- A reverse clock that runs counter-clockwise (time doesn't come back, it just goes)
- Your deadlines with honest assessments of whether you'll make it (you probably won't)
- How much time you actually have left today, this week, this year (less than you think)
- A focus mode for when you need to stop lying to yourself and just do the work

The tone is dry. The assessments are honest. The humor is dark. Because time is serious, and pretending otherwise doesn't make it less true.

---

## Philosophy

People are terrible at feeling the weight of disappearing time. We live in a perpetual "later" that never arrives.

This app makes that feeling visible. It won't help you manage your time better. It won't motivate you with positive affirmations. It will just tell you the truth, with enough dark humor to make looking at it bearable.

The backward clock isn't a gimmick. It's the whole philosophy: time is always running out, you might as well watch it go. The numbers are mirrored because the way we experience time is backwards—we think we have more than we do, until we don't.

---

## Features

- **Reverse Clock** - Analog clock, hands move counter-clockwise. Real time, displayed backward. Because that's how it feels.
- **Deadline Cards** - Shows days/hours remaining with honest urgency assessments. Red means you're in trouble. You probably are.
- **Time Analytics** - Real calculated stats with dry commentary. The year is almost over. Again.
- **Focus Mode** - Fullscreen countdown. 90 minutes. No excuses. Just work.
- **Command Palette** - Press `⌘K` to access commands. Because clicking is for people with time to waste.
- **Widget Mode** - For your second monitor. So you can stare at your mortality while you work.
