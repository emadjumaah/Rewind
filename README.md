# Rewind

A real-time deadline dashboard for people who are bad at feeling how much time they have left.

Not a task manager. Not a productivity tool. Just an honest mirror with a dark sense of humor.

---

## What it is

Time is always running out. You might as well watch it go.

Rewind shows you:
- A reverse clock that runs counter-clockwise (time doesn't come back)
- Your deadlines with honest assessments of whether you'll make it
- How much time you actually have left today, this week, this year
- A focus mode for when you need to stop lying to yourself

The tone is dry. The assessments are honest. The humor is dark.

---

## Stack

React, Vite, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion, Zustand, date-fns, Lucide React.

---

## Getting started

```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open http://localhost:5173

---

## Philosophy

People are bad at feeling the weight of disappearing time. This app makes that feeling visible.

It won't help you manage your time better. It won't motivate you. It will just tell you the truth, with enough dark humor to make looking at it bearable.

The backward clock isn't a gimmick. It's the whole philosophy: time is always running out, you might as well watch it go.

---

## Features

- **Reverse Clock** - Analog clock, hands move counter-clockwise. Real time, displayed backward.
- **Deadline Cards** - Shows days/hours remaining with honest urgency assessments.
- **Time Analytics** - Real calculated stats with dry commentary.
- **Focus Mode** - Fullscreen countdown. 90 minutes. No excuses.
- **Command Palette** - Press `⌘K` to access commands.
